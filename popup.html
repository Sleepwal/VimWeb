Vim Web

v0.1

Navigation

  • j Scroll Down
  • k Scroll Up
  • h Scroll Left
  • l Scroll Right
  • gg Scroll to Top
  • G Scroll to Bottom
  • Ctrl+d Half Page Down
  • Ctrl+u Half Page Up

Interaction

  • f Hint Mode (Click)
  • Space Click at Cursor
  • q / Q Go Back
  • Esc Exit Mode

Keyboard is the world.
